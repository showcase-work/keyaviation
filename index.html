WHO IS KEY AVIATION

Key Aviation’s focus is on purchasing older airplanes, updating them, and then selling these planes for a profit, very similar to the process of flipping real estate. This task provides invest- ment opportunities for those looking for a safe, high yield investment.
Key Aviation identifies sellers and buyers of aircraft, as well as investors.

Key Aviation is based out of San Bernardino, California and will partner with Stratus Aircraft Services, Inc. who will do the upgrades and maintenance work on each aircraft. Stratus Aircraft Services is certified with the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) under Title 14 of the Code of Federal Regula- tions and specializes in aircraft main- tenance, modification, maintenance support, inspection for commercial, military, regional, business, and trans- port. The company has earned a global reputation for repair quality, dependable turnaround times and competitive pricing.

Salomon Zirdok is the founder and CEO of Key Aviation. He created the Company when he saw that indivi- duals who wanted to purchase an air- craft, but had limited resources, were unable to obtain access to the aero- nautic market. This concept provides not just the product, but fills a need within the marketplace.

Salomon Zirdok not new to business ownership or operations. He launched his first entrepreneurial endeavor at the age of 20. Since his start, he has created and improved four additional successful businesses across a variety of industries. Today, Zirdok provides services throughout Mexico and the United States.
