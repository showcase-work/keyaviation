Investment Opportunity

INVESTMENT THE BUYING PROCESS
  • 1. Investment Opportunity
  • 2. Dividend Agreement
  • 3. Deposit to the Company
  • 4. Escrow Deposit
  • 5. Airplane title and transportation to the facility
  • 6. Remodeling and Maintenance
  • 7. Sale/Lease
  • 8. Distribution of Profits
The investments will be divided by the risk associated as well as the time period of the investment.
The turn-around time for this process will vary, depending on the work needed on the aircraft, but should always be within two to six months. This time frame will be establi- shed by a third party company that will measure the investment specifications.

Once the investment is completed and the return is received, the profits will be deposited to investors or reinvested at their discretion.
